## Sprites

Pokemon sprite image files are not committed to this repository.

The app can optionally use locally downloaded sprites in `assets/sprites/`.
Those files are ignored by git. PokeAPI's sprite repository states that image
contents are copyright The Pokemon Company, so users should review upstream
rights and licensing before redistributing builds that include sprites.

Source:

- https://github.com/PokeAPI/sprites
